# One-Day Marathon Prep Meal Plan for 68 kg Athlete

## Nutritional Targets
- **Calories:** ~3,200-3,400 kcal
- **Carbohydrates:** 55-60% (~475-520g) - for glycogen loading
- **Protein:** 1.6-2.0g/kg (~110-135g)
- **Fat:** 20-25% (~70-95g)

---

## BREAKFAST (6:00-7:00 AM)
**Pre-Long Run Meal** | *~800 kcal | 140g carbs | 25g protein*

- 2 cups oatmeal with water
- 1 banana, sliced
- 2 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp almond butter
- 1 cup low-fat Greek yogurt
- Pinch of salt

**Hydration:** 400ml water + electrolytes

---

## MID-MORNING SNACK (10:00-10:30 AM)
**During/Post-Run Fuel** | *~350 kcal | 85g carbs | 8g protein*

- Sports drink (500ml) OR
- 2 energy gels + 500ml water
- 1 medium apple

---

## LUNCH (1:00-2:00 PM)
**Recovery & Carb Loading** | *~850 kcal | 145g carbs | 45g protein*

- 250g grilled chicken breast
- 2 cups cooked white rice (refined carbs for faster absorption)
- Mixed vegetables (broccoli, carrots, bell peppers) - 2 cups
- 1 tbsp olive oil for cooking
- Small side salad with lemon dressing

---

## AFTERNOON SNACK (4:00-4:30 PM)
**Sustained Energy** | *~400 kcal | 85g carbs | 15g protein*

- 2 slices whole grain bread
- 2 tbsp peanut butter
- 1 tbsp jam
- 1 cup low-fat milk
- Handful of pretzels (30g)

---

## DINNER (7:00-8:00 PM)
**Final Carb Load** | *~900 kcal | 155g carbs | 40g protein*

- 200g lean ground turkey (93/7)
- 2 cups cooked pasta (white or whole wheat)
- 1.5 cups marinara sauce
- 1 slice garlic bread
- Side of steamed broccoli (1.5 cups)
- Small green salad with Italian dressing

---

## EVENING SNACK (9:30 PM)
**Sleep Support** | *~250 kcal | 50g carbs | 12g protein*

- 1 cup chocolate milk (low-fat)
- 1 rice cake with 1 tbsp honey
- 10 almonds

---

## HYDRATION STRATEGY
- **Throughout day:** 3-4 liters total
- **Timing:** 500ml with each meal + 200-300ml with snacks
- **Electrolytes:** Include sodium in sports drinks (especially post-run)
- **Evening:** 300ml water 2 hours before bed

---

## KEY NOTES FOR MARATHON PREP

✅ **High carb content** (55-60%) maximizes muscle glycogen storage
✅ **Refined carbs** (white rice, pasta) near workouts for faster absorption
✅ **Adequate protein** supports muscle repair and adaptation
✅ **Evenly distributed carbs** throughout day (not clustered)
✅ **Sodium inclusion** aids hydration retention
✅ **Low fiber before/after runs** reduces GI distress
✅ **Practiced nutrition** - athlete should test this plan during training, not race day
