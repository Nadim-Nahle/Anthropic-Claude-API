# One-Day Marathon Preparation Meal Plan
**Athlete Profile:** 180 cm, 68 kg | Goal: Carbohydrate Loading for Endurance

---

## Daily Nutrition Target
- **Calories:** ~3,200-3,400 kcal
- **Carbohydrates:** 520-580g (65-70% of intake)
- **Protein:** 102-136g (15-20% of intake)
- **Fat:** 85-113g (10-15% of intake)

---

## MEAL PLAN

### **BREAKFAST** (6:30-7:00 AM)
*Pre-training fuel – easily digestible carbs*
- 2 cups oatmeal with honey and banana
- 1 tbsp almond butter
- 8 oz low-fat milk
- 1 medium orange

**Macros:** 550 cal | 105g carbs | 15g protein | 8g fat

---

### **Pre-Workout Snack** (1.5 hours before training, ~9:00 AM)
*Quick energy boost*
- 1 energy bar (oats/honey based)
- 16 oz sports drink (6% carbs)

**Macros:** 280 cal | 68g carbs | 4g protein | 2g fat

---

### **LUNCH** (12:30-1:00 PM)
*Post-workout recovery + continued carb loading*
- 1.5 cups white rice or pasta
- 150g grilled chicken breast
- 1 cup roasted vegetables (carrots, zucchini)
- 1 tbsp olive oil (cooking)
- Side salad with light vinaigrette

**Macros:** 820 cal | 125g carbs | 48g protein | 12g fat

---

### **Afternoon Snack** (4:00 PM)
*Sustained energy*
- Large bagel with 2 tbsp jam
- 1 medium apple with 1 tbsp peanut butter

**Macros:** 450 cal | 72g carbs | 12g protein | 11g fat

---

### **DINNER** (6:30-7:00 PM)
*Final carb load + quality protein*
- 2 cups cooked sweet potato
- 150g lean ground turkey (93% lean)
- 1.5 cups steamed broccoli with 1 tsp butter
- Small whole wheat roll

**Macros:** 750 cal | 115g carbs | 42g protein | 10g fat

---

### **Evening Snack** (Before bed, ~9:30 PM)
*Sleep-friendly carbs + slow-digesting protein*
- 1 cup Greek yogurt (non-fat)
- 1/2 cup granola
- Handful of berries

**Macros:** 280 cal | 45g carbs | 17g protein | 3g fat

---

## HYDRATION PROTOCOL
- **6:30 AM - 9:00 AM:** 500 mL water + 250 mL sports drink during workout
- **Throughout day:** 3-4 liters of water (1 liter per meal period)
- **Evening:** 500 mL water with electrolytes

---

## KEY STRATEGIES FOR MARATHON PREP

✓ **Carbohydrate timing** around training window  
✓ **High glycemic index carbs** (white rice, bagels, sports drinks) for rapid absorption  
✓ **Adequate protein** (1.5-2g per kg body weight) for muscle repair  
✓ **Consistent portions** to prevent GI distress during race  
✓ **Hydration emphasis** critical for endurance training  

**Note:** Consume this plan 2-3 days before the marathon while reducing training volume.
